Privacy Policy for Nodes

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Nodes is an independent iOS app for building visual AI image workflows using a user-provided fal.ai API key.

Information We Collect

Nodes does not require users to create an account.

The app may store your fal.ai API key locally on your device so you can use AI generation features. Your API key is used only to make requests to fal.ai from the app.

Nodes uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostic information. This may include device information, app version, operating system version, crash logs, and other technical data needed to diagnose and fix app issues.

AI Requests

When you use AI generation or editing features, prompts, images, and related workflow data may be sent to fal.ai in order to process your request. Nodes is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by fal.ai.

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Provide AI generation and editing features
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app reliability
  • Respond to support requests

Third-Party Services

Nodes uses the following third-party services:

  • fal.ai for AI generation and processing, when you provide an API key and use AI features
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics for crash reporting and diagnostics

Your use of these services may be subject to their own privacy policies and terms.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be processed by third-party services only as needed to provide app functionality, crash reporting, diagnostics, and support.

Data Retention

Crash reports and diagnostic data may be retained by Google Firebase according to Firebase’s policies. AI requests may be processed and retained by fal.ai according to fal.ai’s policies. Locally stored app data remains on your device unless you delete it or uninstall the app.

Children’s Privacy

Nodes is not intended for children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
